--- layout: default title: "Character Creation" ---

Stats

For your character's primary stats, roll 5 + 1d6 three times. Then assign the results, in any order, to Strength, Agility, and Psyche.

Career

Determine your starting career.

Gain the career's starting skill ranks.

Gain the career's starting equipment.

Gain the career's starting perk(s).

Skills

Gain a number of skill points equal to your Intelligence.

Spend each skill point to increase your rank in a skill by one.

Each rank grants a +4 bonus to relevant tests.

Perks

Select one available perk.
