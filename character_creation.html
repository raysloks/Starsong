Stats

For your character's primary stats, roll 3d6 three times, using the middle value plus 5 as the result. You may then assign each of the results to either Strength, Agility, or Mind.

Example

Billy rolls 3d6. The dice land on 4, 3, and 6. The middle value is 4, so Billy notes down 9 as the result.

He then rolls 1, 5, and 5, noting 10 as the result.

Then, he unfortunately rolls 3, 1, and 1, noting 6 as the result.

Finally, he assigns 10 to his Strength, 6 to his Agility, and 9 to his Mind.
